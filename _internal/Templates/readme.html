Log Bot for "ARK Ascended"

This application monitors 'Tribe Logs' and provides a general overview of in-game activity without requiring the game window to be active.

Example

With "ARK Ascended" running in the background, you are able to play other games/browse while keeping logs completely covered.

Installation/Setup

  • Launch RavenLog Installer
  • Install the 64bit version of Tesseract. Tesseract Installation
  • After Tesseract installs, you will have to set up your Discord Webhooks (2-3). Guide on setting up Discord Webhooks
  • Get the "roleid" of the Roles that you want to ping in Discord. Guide on getting role ID

settings.ini Configuration

Log_Alert
  alert_urls = Live Updates
  log_urls = General Update
  ocr_urls = Image-Text

Roles
  roles = Main Role ID to Ping
  svroles = Server Specific Role

Running the bot

Open RavenLog.exe and follow the instructions. If everything is set up correctly, you should see the application monitoring logs as expected.

Note: Please use the default game INI for the best experience.

Alert Descriptions

Here’s what each alert in the application signifies:

  • Kills: Notifies you when your tribe has eliminated another player or creature. (Works well with named turrets)
  • Death & Destruction: Alerts you to any losses within your tribe, such as deaths or destroyed structures.
  • Parasaur Ping: Triggered when your Parasaur detects an enemy nearby, ensuring you're aware of potential threats.
  • Starvation: Informs you if one of your dinosaurs has died due to lack of food, helping you manage your creatures better.
  • Demolition: Notifies you when someone in your tribe has demolished one of your own structures, useful for keeping track of changes within your base.
  • Enemy Structure Destroyed: Reports when your tribe has demolished an enemy’s structure, keeping you informed of offensive activities.
  • Log Positioning: Indicates potential issues with the log monitoring setup, such as the logs not appearing where expected, which may necessitate adjustments for accurate tracking.
